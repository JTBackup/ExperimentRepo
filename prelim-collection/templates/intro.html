Welcome to Our Study

We are reaching out to invite you to participate in an exciting study exploring the realms of digital media and social media experiences. This study is being conducted by Gerald Cupchik, Professor at The University of Toronto Scarborough and we are interested in understanding how digital platforms and social media impact our daily lives and shape our interactions within the digital world.

Please, keep in mind that this is a two part study designed to investigate your lived experiences and perceptions around social media and privacy through both open-ended audio-visual questions and responses to a series of standard scales. For this part of the study, you are required to have access to both a microphone and camera so that you can provide you audio-visual responses. In addition, the study must be taken from a desktop or computer on Google Chrome as the study does not support mobile devices or other browsers. Please, do not continue if you do not currently have access to a computer with Google Chrome, a microphone, and a webcam.
