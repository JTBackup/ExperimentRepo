Welcome to Our Study

Important: This is a two-part study designed to investigate your lived experiences and perceptions around social media and privacy through both open-ended audio-visual questions and responses to a series of standard scales.

For this part of the study, you are required to have access to both a microphone and camera so that you can provide your audio-visual responses. In addition, the study must be taken from a desktop or laptop computer using Google Chrome, as the study does not support mobile devices or other browsers.

Please do not continue if you do not currently have access to a computer with Google Chrome, a microphone, and a webcam.
